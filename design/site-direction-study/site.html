03 / Method

The work is one continuous line.

Strategy, interface, implementation, and proof stay connected. That is how small builds avoid becoming expensive handoff chains.

  1. 01
    Find the real constraint

    Strip the request down to the decision, bottleneck, or repeated pain that actually matters.

    Diagnose
  2. 02
    Make the shape visible

    Prototype full workflows with real content before production code hardens the wrong idea.

    Design
  3. 03
    Build the narrowest useful thing

    Choose architecture around the proof path, not around imaginary scale.

    Implement
  4. 04
    Prove and operate it

    Ship with verification, observability, and a clear next decision instead of a launch-day cliff.

    Verify
