02

Working method

One continuous line from question to proof.

Strategy, interface, implementation, and verification stay connected. That keeps a small build from turning into an expensive chain of handoffs.

  1. 01
    DIAGNOSEFind the real constraint.

    Strip the request down to the decision, bottleneck, or repeated pain that actually matters.

    Clarity before scope
  2. 02
    DESIGNMake the shape visible.

    Prototype the full workflow with real content before production code hardens the wrong idea.

    Behavior before polish
  3. 03
    IMPLEMENTBuild the narrowest useful thing.

    Choose architecture around the proof path and the operator—not around imaginary scale.

    Useful before broad
  4. 04
    VERIFYProve it in the real world.

    Ship with evidence, observability, and a clear next decision instead of a launch-day cliff.

    Receipts before claims
