DESIGN DECISIONS / 01

Field Journal, made quieter and more expensive.

The premium signal comes from scale, rhythm, materials, and proof—not shine.
  1. 01
    Editorial scale

    The Newsreader display face now has enough room to feel intentional. Instrument Sans carries every functional detail at a readable size.

  2. 02
    Ink + brass

    Near-black paper, warm bone text, aged brass, and quiet sage. No bright neon and no gradient headline.

  3. 03
    Commissioned proof

    Each case gets a bespoke product artifact and a constraint → response narrative instead of a generic card.

  4. 04
    Ultra-wide aware

    The canvas grows to 1,980px with larger reading sizes on wide displays, while keeping line lengths controlled.

  5. 05
    Quiet motion

    Scroll reveals and progress are restrained, optional, and fully removed for reduced-motion users.
